Rules and Regulations

1.After filling out this registration form, your initial registration has been completed and this is not the final registration. It is mandatory for you to come to the practice location and register after the practice starts.
2.After filling the registration form, you will be given further instructions from time to time via WHATSAPP.
3.The team's practice will begin soon and all information related to it will be provided. You can complete your final registration before the practice begins.
4.Since space in the team is limited, priority will be given on a first-come, first-served basis.
5.No registration will be accepted under any pretext after all seats are filled.
6.Strict adherence to all instructions and rules given by the team is mandatory.
7.Practice and practice time are mandatory for everyone.
8.If you have any problems with the team's practice times due to work or other constraints, please contact us.
9.The team may cancel the registration at any time and the team will not be obligated to answer anyone for this.
